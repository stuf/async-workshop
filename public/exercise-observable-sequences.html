Observable sequences

Time to focus on Observable sequences. Simple, one-off values are great for their own specific things, but in real-world applications we need something more.

A sequence is like the name implies, a sequence of events, or values, one after each other. In practice, an Observable sequence requires us to modify an Observable into a new Observable. It may sound confusing, but Kefir allows us to do this in a number of ways.
